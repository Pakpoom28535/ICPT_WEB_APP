ICPT 2025 Abstract Guidelines

Content

  • Language: English
  • Length: 150 no more than 250 words
  • Visuals: May include ONE photograph, graph, or diagram (no tables)
  • Structure:
    • State the purpose of your research
    • Highlight the main findings
    • Summarize key conclusions
    • Include up to 5 keywords

Submission

  • Template: Download the provided template (ICPT2025 Template.)
  • EasyChair Submission:
    • Log in or create an EasyChair account (EasyChair Login.)
    • Fill in author details, title, abstract, and keywords
    • Upload your abstract file (Only doc.x, PDF)
    • Review and submit
    • You may walk through the steps on EasyChair by following the link : EasyChair Guidelines.

Post-Submission

  • Track your submission status through your conference account (Submission Progress.)
